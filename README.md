# The Oracle of Seating

A mystical HTML oracle that divines where you should sit, based on the day of the week and time of day. Because apparently your seating arrangements are too important to leave to chance.

Features:
- **Cosmic divination** — Real-time prophecies powered by Copenhagen timezone magic
- **Theme shifting** — Watch the UI transform as the oracle's mood changes throughout the week
- **Particle effects** — Ambient floating dots that vibe with your destiny
- **Confetti chaos** — Midweek celebrations nobody asked for
- **Debug preview** — Skip ahead through time to see what the oracle has in store for you
- **No refunds** — Seating assignments are final

Perfect for teams that take their desk assignments *way* too seriously.
